## Overview

Georgia uses a **flat 5.49% tax rate** on all taxable income. Understanding how Georgia's tax system works helps you accurately predict your paycheck and plan your finances throughout the year.

## 2025 Tax Rates

Georgia taxes all taxable income at a flat rate of **5.49%**. This applies equally to all income levels — a $30,000 earner and a $300,000 earner pay the same percentage of their taxable income.

## Standard Deduction

Georgia provides a state standard deduction of **$12,000** for single filers and **$24,000** for married filing jointly. This amount is subtracted from gross income before your state tax rate is applied, lowering your overall state tax burden.

## Who Must File in Georgia?

Generally, any resident of Georgia who earns income above the filing threshold must file a state income tax return. The Georgia Department of Revenue sets annual filing thresholds, which roughly correspond to the standard deduction amounts.

## How Georgia Tax Affects Your Paycheck

Your employer withholds Georgia state income tax from each paycheck based on your W-4 equivalent form and expected annual income. On a $75,000 annual salary, a single filer in Georgia can expect to pay approximately $2,940–$3,459 in state income taxes annually.

## Tips to Reduce Your Georgia State Tax Bill

- **Contribute to a pre-tax 401(k)** — reduces both your federal and state taxable income
- **Maximize HSA and FSA contributions** if available through your employer
- **Claim all applicable deductions** — Georgia may allow deductions beyond the standard deduction
- **Review your withholding** each year to avoid underpayment penalties

## Filing Deadlines

Georgia's state income tax return is generally due on **April 15**, aligning with the federal deadline. An automatic extension to October 15 is usually available, but any tax owed must still be paid by April 15 to avoid penalties.


## How Georgia Compares to Neighboring States

Georgia borders Florida, Alabama, Tennessee, and others. Compared to its neighbors, Georgia's flat 5.49% rate places it in the middle of the pack for states in the region.
